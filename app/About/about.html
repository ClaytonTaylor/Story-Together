Who We Are

We are a team of Story-Tellers that are passionate about Stories. More specifically, the stories that go untold. We believe that the Era of a Studio-Dominated Film Industry is over. With the introduction of the internet, we now have the capability to tell our own stories. But there is a problem every Filmmaker faces.

Problem

Unless you work for a studio or have a large list of connections, your path to the "Big Screen" is severley limited by who you know. Filmmaking is a Team Effort, but what if you don't know any Writers, or Camera Operators, Costume Designers, or even Grips? You are forced doing jobs you don't specialize in and, as a result, don't focus your speciality and the overall quality of your Story dimenishes. Or, you spend days, weeks, or even months looking for the right people for the right job.

No Longer

Solution

Call Time is an application that takes your data and generates a completed list of other Story-Tellers around you with different skillsets. Now, find others instantly, connect, and begin the Story-Telling process. Stop wasting time looking for other Filmmakers, and begin creating the dreams you've always wanted to watch.
